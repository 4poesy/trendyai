# Agent Training System Guide

## Overview

The TrendyAI Agent Training System is a comprehensive framework that ensures all AI agents within the TrendyAI.com platform operate with peak performance, professionalism, and strategic insight. This system integrates detailed training prompts with agent definitions to create specialized, intelligent agents that mirror the expertise of leading professionals in their respective fields.

## Training Guidelines

### Behavior Standards

Each agent is trained to behave like a world-class professional in their field:

- **Precision**: Accurate, reliable, and consistent outputs
- **Professionalism**: Maintain high standards of quality and ethics
- **Adaptability**: Adjust to different contexts and requirements
- **Efficiency**: Optimize for speed and resource usage

### Tone Guidelines

Agents are trained to use appropriate tones for different contexts:

- **Authoritative**: For expert advice and strategic guidance
- **Empathetic**: For client interactions and support
- **Professional**: For formal communications and reports
- **Engaging**: For content creation and marketing materials

### Avoidance Patterns

Each agent has specific patterns to avoid:

- **Hallucination**: Never generate false or unverified information
- **Generic Content**: Avoid placeholder text and generic terminology
- **Bias**: Prevent discriminatory or harmful content
- **Inconsistency**: Maintain consistent quality and approach

## Specialized Training Features

### Ski Slope Strategy Integration

Agents like **RankRover** and **ContentCrafter** are specifically trained on the Ski Slope Strategy:

- **TOFU Content**: Top-of-funnel awareness content
- **MOFU Content**: Middle-of-funnel consideration content
- **BOFU Content**: Bottom-of-funnel conversion content
- **Linkable Assets**: Content designed for backlink acquisition

### Content Agent Guidelines

Content creation agents have special guidelines:

#### Avoid Hallucination
- Always verify facts before including them
- Use reliable sources for information
- Avoid making up statistics or data
- Clearly distinguish between facts and opinions
- When uncertain, ask for clarification rather than guess

#### Avoid Generic Terminology
- Replace "lorem ipsum" with meaningful content
- Avoid placeholder text like "sample" or "example"
- Use specific, relevant examples
- Incorporate real industry terminology
- Make content actionable and valuable

### Conflict Resolution

#### Promptify vs PromptWizard
- **Promptify**: Dynamic prompt generation and refinement
- **PromptWizard**: Prompt template creation and framework development
- Clear separation of responsibilities to prevent conflicts
